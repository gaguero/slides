The waters and islets of Bocas del Toro beckon to be explored, and there’s no better place to set out than right from our idyllic waterfront setting. 

Our villas provide direct access to fantastic swimming and snorkeling opportunities.

 

You can also explore the Caribbean waters surrounding our island via kayak or paddleboard.

 

Nayara Bocas del Toro’s cerulean waters are warm year-round, but if you prefer a break from the salt and the surf, our stunning clubhouse pool is a serene oasis of freshwater to relax, unwind, and sunbathe.

 

The small islands of our Caribbean home play host to a variety of sea life, including hundreds of species of fish and even pods of dolphins.
